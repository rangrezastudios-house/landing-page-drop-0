The silhouette philosophy

Is it a shirt?
Is it a kurta?
It's a Shurta.

An all-occasion silhouette inspired by Indian lifestyle and fashion. Traditionally, locals across India preferred a form of the kurta as their go-to attire — from the tribals of Rajasthan to farmers in the far south. It's a unified silhouette that comforts the entire Indian diaspora.

We're reimagining it as a tribute to Indian fashion — putting it on the map in a market dominated by the borrowed silhouettes of shirts and t-shirts. The Shurta is our very own desi silhouette for the world.

We are a Shurta-first brand — every piece takes this India-inspired silhouette further into the world.

The founder · Jaipur
Our story

House of Rangreza is an honest expression of an artist, an entrepreneur, a traveller.

It's a boutique studio from Jaipur — and an ode to the artisan community that has dyed and printed this city's cloth for centuries. A way to carry that craft forward: documented, preserved, and made into limited pieces of the highest quality.

Not just another textile house from Jaipur — but a way to bring its living heritage to amazing people like you. Drop 0 is where it begins.

Thank you for your trust in us, much love.

~ Rangreza
